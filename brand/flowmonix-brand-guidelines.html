06 ΟΔβ²§βÄù Brand Voice

Tone & Messaging

FlowMonix is technical but not cold. Confident but not arrogant. The language of a senior engineer who has been on-call.

Precise

"3 workflows failed in the last 15 minutes."

Not "some issues detected." Be specific with numbers, timestamps, and workflow names.

Calm

"Incident auto-resolved. No action needed."

Reduce noise. Every alert must be actionable. No false urgency ΟΔβ²§βÄù operators trust the system.

Empowering

"Retry execution" not "Try again."

Action labels are verbs. Users are in control. The interface never blames the user.
